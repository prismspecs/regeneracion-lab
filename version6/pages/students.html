Study & Research Opportunities

The Regeneración Lab offers opportunities for students to engage in community-based research, decolonial praxis, and collaborative scholarship. We work with undergraduate and graduate students and postdoctoral fellows across disciplines who are committed to research justice and critical-creative methodologies.

Ways to Engage

Students can participate through directed research projects, reading groups and study circles, workshops, and collaborative initiatives with our resident scholars. We prioritize hands-on learning that centers community needs and Indigenous and marginalized epistemologies.

Resources

Access our Indigenous Border Studies Syllabus, research justice frameworks, and educational materials developed through our community-based projects. These resources are designed to support critical engagement with decolonial scholarship and praxis.
