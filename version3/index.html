ABOUT THE LAB

An anti-colonial project of Indigenous nihilism, for a non-European anarchy.

Regeneración Lab brings together scholars, artists, and activists working at the intersections of Indigenous studies, border politics, and decolonial theory.

Named after the anarchist newspaper published by the Flores Magón brothers from 1900-1918, our lab carries forward their spirit of radical critique while centering Indigenous epistemologies often erased in traditional anarchist narratives.

We create space for research, dialogue, and cultural production that challenges settler-colonial frameworks and imagines otherwise.
